Privacy Policy

This policy covers the website alimahouk.com. Any information you submit to this website will never be shared with any third parties without your explicit permission. Be aware that some functions of the website are designed for sharing information publicly, in which case both registered users and unregistered visitors may be able to view that information.

Use of Cookies

Cliques uses cookies to store session data linked to the computer user. This data is used to determine whether a user is logged in or not.

Security

The website takes every precaution to protect any sensitive information submitted by users. Passwords are always stored and transmitted in hashed form. In addition, the website employs Transport Layer Security (TLS) to establish secure connections between a user's computer and Cliques servers.

Compliance With Local Law Enforcement

The IP addresses of every user are logged for the purposes of website governance. In the event of a user breaching the Cliques terms of use by commiting actions deemed illegal under the laws of the United Kingdom, we will co-operate with local law enforcement and share identifying information of the user in question with the concerned law enforcement agencies.

Changes in Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated clearly to all users.
